Kendrick Lamar's Going on The DAMN. Tour

By Jared McNett  |  April 24, 2017  |  10:25am
Photo by Christopher Polk/Getty Music News Kendrick Lamar
Share Tweet Submit Pin

In the past two weeks, Kendrick Lamar has debuted one of the best-reviewed albums of 2017, earned yet another number one album on the Billboard charts, and played two headlining spots at Coachella.

Now Lamar’s going out for more performances, and he’s bringing Travis Scott and D.R.A.M. along with him. The tour kickstarts in July in Phoenix and runs through early August, concluding in the rapper’s hometown of Los Angeles. At Coachella, Lamar debuted multiple songs from DAMN. and will likely debut several more once he’s out on the road. He and Scott have the song “goosebumps” together, so that’ll surely make an appearance on the tour at some point.

Check out the dates for Lamar’s The DAMN. Tour below—tickets go on sale this Friday, April 28. While waiting, you can also check out Lamar’s mom’s review of his album below and watch the still-bonkers clip for the blistering “DNA.” right here.
